BEER BREAD

Serves: 6-8

  • Ingredients
  • 2 sticks butter (not margarine)
  • 1 tablespoon celery seed
  • 1 - 16 ounce loaf white bakery bread, unsliced, ordered in advance from bakery
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • Directions
  • Preheat oven to 425°
  • Cream together butter, celery seed and garlic salt
  • Mixture should be soft but not melted
  • Cut off crust on sides and top of bread, but leave bottom crust
  • Cut lengthwise twice (into thirds) but do not cut through bottom
  • Butter inside of cuts with butter mixture
  • Cut across width of loaf 6 to 8 times buttering the cuts as you go
  • Frost outside of the loaf with remaining butter mixture
  • Place on piece of foil on cookie sheet and bake uncovered 12 to 15 minutes or until crisp and barely golden
